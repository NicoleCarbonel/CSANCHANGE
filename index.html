About Us

With petitions that give voices to the students of City of San Jose Del Monte National Science Highschool, CSanChange is a learner-oriented platform for proposing change and creating benefecial impact through establishing petitions and campaigns

Start a Petition

Starting a petition with CSanChange is simple. Simply follow our online process, linked below, and within minutes your petition will be live. Whether you act individually or on behalf of a class, CSanChange has the right tools and social media environment for your campaign.

Build Support

CSanChange provides a variety of tools to build support for the student's campaign. Social media channels are integrated into each petition while list building options are available with our management features.

Promote Campaigns

CSanChange can deliver real results for your cause. We list a variety of testimonials which reflect both campaign successes and student's satisfaction with our features. Our aim is not simply to amplify the voices of individual students and groups but to deliver positive outcomes for our representatives.

Follow and Advocate Causes

CSanChange is not just a website for supporting campaigns but to advocate causes in CSANSCI. This means that students can follow and connect with a variety of other members who have similar interests and values. CSanChange ecosystem encourages mutual sharing of ideas and support to facilitate positive outcomes.
